# Methodology

Fill this in. Your AI tools read this file to write your pipeline, so be specific.

## 1. What goes in?
Datasets, years, geographic scope. How they join (the key that connects them). What one row represents. How many observations you expect.

## 2. What comes out?
Your outcome variable. Continuous, categorical, ranking, cluster, or time series? One sentence: "I am predicting ___ from ___."

## 3. What features matter?
Raw columns vs. engineered features. Flag which features are novel; that is often the publishable contribution.

## 4. What models and why?
Model choice and why, plus a baseline to compare against.

## 5. How do you know it worked?
Split strategy, metrics, and what a good result looks like for your question.
